Announcements

This is a collection of our press announcements including new features, presentations, public meetings and general important information.

NewPip Beta now available

February 16, 2018, TobiGr

With the aim of making NewPipe bug free and achieving a stable 1.0.0 release, we are proud to announce the immediate availability of NewPipe Beta.

The beta is provided as a separate app and can be downloaded and installed via F-Droid. It includes not only new features such as local playlist and SoundCloud support. Furthermore, it comes with an interface to detect memory leaks.

From now on, Team NewPipe try to ensure to only introduce new stable features to the normal application.

Team NewPipe start blog

December 17, 2017, TheAssassin

After two years of development, we decided to keep you up a little better about what we do. Therefore, today, we introduce our new blog, which will be used to announce releases, changes, and keep you up with all the cool stuff we work on.

Since we started this blog only recently, we haven’t decided on how to use it, and whether we consider publishing third-party posts. Therefore, feel free to contact us with ideas and/or texts. See this page for information how to get in touch with us.

Special thanks goes to Tobias Groza, who has set up the blog system and put a lot of effort in the design.
